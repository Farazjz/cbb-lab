About

The Computational Biology and Biomedicine Lab (CBB Lab) advances research at the intersection of biology, medicine, and computational science. We develop and share in silico models that help explore complex cellular, molecular, and biomedical systems.

Our mission is to foster open, collaborative, and reproducible research in computational biology and biomedicine.

Research Focus

  • Cellular and Molecular Simulations: Modeling biological systems to understand complex interactions.
  • Biomedical Modeling: Simulating physiological processes and disease dynamics.
  • Systems Biology: Integrating data-driven and mechanistic models for biological insight.
  • AI and Computational Methods: Applying machine learning to biomedical challenges.
